Thesis

Online gambling has become a serious social issue in Indonesia. With easy access through the internet and mobile devices, more and more people, especially the younger generation, are getting involved in online gambling activities.

Arguments

One big reason why online gambling is spreading fast is because internet access is everywhere. Even children and teenagers can open gambling websites anytime. Many parents do not watch what their kids do online, making it easier for them to find these sites. Another cause is money problems. People want fast money, and they think gambling is the answer. Also, there is not enough education about the dangers of gambling, so people don't understand how risky and harmful it can be.

Conclusion

Online gambling is a real danger to young people and society. We all need to work together to stop it before it causes more harm and destroys the future of our nation.
